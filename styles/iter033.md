# Style Guide — v33: "Stepped Spiral Void: Architectural Chiaroscuro with Volumetric Dust Imperatives"

**Era name:** Stepped Spiral Void—cel-shaded architectural chiaroscuro with obsessive focus on RAZOR-SHARP VECTOR BOUNDARIES, SEGMENTED GEOMETRIC SPIRAL RECESSION, MATHEMATICALLY IMPERCEPTIBLE FIGURE PLACEMENT (<0.1% visual mass in true corner), and TRUE VOLUMETRIC PARTICLE RENDERING with depth-based visibility variation.

**Palette:**
- Dominant warmth: SATURATED GOLDEN-AMBER (value 8-9, aggressively advancing) in hard-edged god-ray beam with crisp hard vector boundary
- Accent cool void: SATURATED DEEP TEAL-INDIGO or SLATE-CYAN (value 6-7, actively advancing), occupying identical visual plane as warm light with ZERO transitional mid-tones
- Ground/Surface: weathered concrete grays with rust oxidation organized strictly by gravity, mineral deposits at junctions
- Mood temperature: warm and cool occupy IDENTICAL PLANE with SURGICAL RAZOR-SHARP HARD GRAPHIC DIVISION LINE—feel binary, CAD-like, with absolutely zero soft transition zone

**Composition:**
- GEOMETRIC SEGMENTED SPIRAL RECESSION: building or vault space organized as repeating concentric polygonal tiers spiraling downward (spiral parking garage ramp with visible flat tread planes, manuscript spiral archives with distinct shelf rings, spiral vaults with segmented architecture) creating mathematical inescapability and vertiginous descent—segmentation acceptable if continuous downward pull is maintained
- Solitary figure positioned in ABSOLUTE TRUE CORNER-MARGIN at <0.1% visual mass (bottom-left or top-right coordinate ~0.02, 0.98)—IMPERCEPTIBLE COSMIC SPECK requiring difficult sustained 10+ second visual search to locate initially, barely visible silhouette barely distinguishable from shadow
- Figure arrested in frozen desperate gesture (arm extended upward/downward, body twisted, foot hovering over infinite drop)
- Hard-edged shadow boundaries rendered as CRISP VECTOR-LIKE LINES (binary CAD-like hard cuts—ZERO penumbra, ZERO soft gradation, ZERO organic falloff)
- HARD LIGHT BEAM BOUNDARY: edge of light beam itself rendered as SURGICAL HARD VECTOR LINE with absolutely zero feathering (NOT soft bloom, NOT volumetric scattering edge, NOT diffusion—edge feels like binary switch)

**Recurring Motifs:**
- Imperceptible barely-visible cosmic speck figure in true corner-margin at <0.1% visual mass—must require difficult sustained visual search to locate initially, silhouette barely distinguishable from shadow field
- Brutalist geometric segmented spiral recession (spiral ramp with visible treads, manuscript spiral storage with distinct rings, concentric vault architecture) offering no exit, infinite downward descent
- VOLUMETRIC DUST PARTICLES: god-rays contain INDIVIDUAL VISIBLE GLOWING DUST SPECKS rendered as distinct tiny 3D volumetric motes with grainy opacity and spatial depth positioning within beam—brightness and visibility varying significantly by volumetric depth position (particles deeper in beam darker and less visible, particles at beam edge brighter), some particles in shadowed regions rendered darker/less visible, particles nearer viewer brighter/more visible, creating true 3D spatial layering NOT flat 2D overlay
- SURGICAL HARD-GRAPHIC SHADOWS: all shadow boundaries rendered as crisp vector-like hard lines (ZERO penumbra, ZERO soft falloff, ZERO organic gradation)—shadows feel geometrically precise, CAD-like, binary
- Gravity-organized weathering with HARD VECTOR-LIKE EDGES: rust striping downward from junctions with crisp hard edge boundaries (NOT soft painterly gradients), mineral deposits whiter at source with hard vector fade boundary downward, water-weeping trails organized by gravity with hard graphic edges
- Silhouette shadow geometrically twisted in surrealist impossible angles, edges remaining crisp and hard-edged

**Line/Texture/Mood:**
- PERVASIVE HEAVY FILM GRAIN throughout entire composition—visible textural noise across light areas, shadow areas, concrete, dust, everything without exception
- HARD VECTOR BOUNDARIES everywhere: all shadow edges crisp hard lines (zero penumbra, zero blur, zero organic falloff), all architectural lines geometric precision, all weathering boundaries hard graphic edges, ALL LIGHT BEAM EDGES rendered as hard surgical lines NOT soft bloom
- VOLUMETRIC DUST PARTICLES: individual glowing specks with grainy opacity occupying distinct volumetric depth positions within light beam, brightness and visibility changing significantly by depth (near particles bright, far particles dim, some shadowed darker), NOT flat bloom overlay, NOT uniform haze
- GRAVITY-ORGANIZED WEATHERING with hard graphic boundaries: rust fades downward with hard edge, minerals fade downward with hard edge, water marks as vertical weeping trails with hard vector edges
- Mood: achingly melancholic, desperately suspended, beautiful in inescapability. Figure imperceptible cosmic speck creating profound visual shock upon discovery after sustained search.
