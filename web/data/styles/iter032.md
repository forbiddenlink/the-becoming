# Style Guide — v32: "Hard-Edged Spiral Void: Surgical Chiaroscuro Precision"

**Era name:** Hard-Edged Spiral Void—surgical hard-graphic chiaroscuro with obsessive focus on RAZOR-SHARP VECTOR BOUNDARIES, GEOMETRIC SPIRAL RECESSION, MATHEMATICALLY IMPERCEPTIBLE FIGURE PLACEMENT, and VOLUMETRIC PARTICLE RENDERING.

**Palette:**
- Dominant warmth: SATURATED GOLDEN-AMBER (value 8-9, aggressively advancing) in volumetric god-rays with crisp hard-edge beam boundary
- Accent cool void: SATURATED DEEP TEAL-INDIGO or SLATE-CYAN (value 6-7, actively advancing), occupying identical visual plane as warm light
- Ground/Surface: weathered concrete grays with rust oxidation organized strictly by gravity, mineral deposits at junctions
- Mood temperature: warm and cool occupy IDENTICAL PLANE with SURGICAL RAZOR-SHARP HARD GRAPHIC DIVISION LINE (no soft transition zone, no photographic falloff)

**Composition:**
- GEOMETRIC SPIRAL RECESSION: building or vault space organized as continuous spiraling descent (spiral parking garage ramp, manuscript spiral archives, spiraling staircase, concentric vault spiral recession) pulling vanishing point infinitely DOWNWARD/INWARD, creating mathematical inescapability and vertigo
- Solitary figure positioned in ABSOLUTE TRUE CORNER MARGIN (bottom-left or top-right at <0.1% visual mass)—IMPERCEPTIBLE COSMIC SPECK requiring difficult sustained 10+ second visual search to locate initially, barely visible silhouette at frame edge
- Figure arrested in frozen desperate gesture (arm extended upward/downward, body twisted, foot hovering over infinite drop)
- Hard-edged shadow boundaries rendered as CRISP VECTOR-LIKE LINES (binary CAD-like hard cuts—ZERO penumbra, ZERO soft gradation, ZERO organic falloff, ZERO photographic blur)
- HARD LIGHT BEAM BOUNDARY: edge of light beam itself rendered as SURGICAL HARD VECTOR LINE with absolutely zero feathering (NOT soft bloom, NOT volumetric scattering edge, NOT diffusion—edge feels like binary switch)

**Recurring Motifs:**
- Imperceptible barely-visible cosmic speck figure in true corner-margin at <0.1% visual mass—must require difficult sustained visual search to locate initially
- Brutalist geometric spiral recession (spiral ramp, manuscript spiral storage, concentric spiral vaults, spiral staircases) offering no exit, infinite downward descent
- VOLUMETRIC DUST PARTICLES: god-rays contain VISIBLE INDIVIDUAL GLOWING DUST SPECKS rendered as distinct tiny 3D volumetric motes with grainy opacity and spatial depth within beam (brightness and visibility varying by volumetric position, some particles shadowed, NOT flat 2D dots, NOT bloom halo)
- SURGICAL HARD-GRAPHIC SHADOWS: all shadow boundaries rendered as crisp vector-like hard lines (ZERO penumbra, ZERO soft falloff, ZERO organic gradation)—shadows feel geometrically precise, CAD-like, binary
- Gravity-organized weathering with HARD VECTOR-LIKE EDGES: rust striping downward from junctions with crisp hard edge boundaries (NOT soft painterly gradients), mineral deposits whiter at source with hard vector fade boundary downward, water-weeping trails organized by gravity with hard graphic edges
- Silhouette shadow geometrically twisted in surrealist impossible angles, edges remaining crisp and hard-edged

**Line/Texture/Mood:**
- PERVASIVE HEAVY FILM GRAIN throughout entire composition—visible textural noise across light areas, shadow areas, concrete, dust, everything
- HARD VECTOR BOUNDARIES everywhere: all shadow edges crisp hard lines (zero penumbra, zero blur, zero organic falloff), all architectural lines geometric precision, all weathering boundaries hard graphic edges, ALL LIGHT BEAM EDGES rendered as hard surgical lines NOT soft bloom
- VOLUMETRIC DUST PARTICLES: individual glowing specks with grainy opacity occupying distinct volumetric positions within light beam, brightness and visibility varying by depth, some particles shadowed, NOT flat bloom overlay
- GRAVITY-ORGANIZED WEATHERING with hard graphic boundaries: rust fades downward with hard edge, minerals fade downward with hard edge, water marks as vertical weeping trails with hard vector edges
- Mood: achingly melancholic, desperately suspended, beautiful in inescapability. Figure imperceptible cosmic speck creating visual shock upon discovery. Warm volumetric light with hard beam edge opposed by hard-edged cool shadow occupying same plane—eternal geometric combat rendered through surgical precision. Infinite spiral recession offers no exit. Gravity-visible weathering proves elapsed time. Extreme corner imperceptibility emphasizes cosmic insignificance. HARD VECTOR PRECISION as PRIMARY signature: all edges rendered as crisp hard graphic lines with ZERO soft transitions, ZERO photographic blur, ZERO organic falloff—edges feel surgically cut, CAD-like, binary.
